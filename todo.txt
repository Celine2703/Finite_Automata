group 6

The text file representing an automaton read by your program may have the following
syntax (but you may prefer a slightly different structure if you wish):
Line 1: the automaton’s alphabet. a,b,c...
Line 2: number of states.
Line 3: is,is,...
Line 4: fs,fs...
Line 5: number of transitions.
Lines 6 and the following lines: transitions in the form
0,a,0
